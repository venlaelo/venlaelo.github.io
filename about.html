About

Venla Elonsalo is a Finnish

fashion and textile designer.

In her work, she combines plush toys to fashion. The work concentrates on comfort and accompany given by plush toys. She collected plush toys when she was a kid, and nowadays she still collects them. They are very important for her.

During her fashion design BA studies in Aalto University, Venla has expanded her knowledge in knitwear, woven fabrics, fabric printing and pattern making. In 2019, Venla visited in The Great Yorkshire Teddy Bear Event to learn more about collectible teddy bears and how to make them. In her Wearable Soft Toys collection, she uses traditional teddy bear mohair to achieve best possible properties for her designs and for the environment. Otherwise, she mostly uses local surplus fabrics.

Furthermore, she has become fascinated by technological possibilities in fashion. Venla enjoys combining these possibilities with a more traditional way of making. For example, she has utilized 3D softwares such as Blender and CLO3D while making patterns
